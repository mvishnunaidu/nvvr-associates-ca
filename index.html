About Us
N V R R & ASSOCIATES,
Chartered Accountants

M/s. N V R R & ASSOCIATES is a professionally managed Chartered Accountancy firm with offices in Hyderabad and Tirupati, registered with the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI).

The firm was constituted on 12th February 2014 and is managed by partners CA. N. Venkata Ramana Reddy (FCA) and CA. Mundla Rajasekhar Reddy (ACA), qualified Chartered Accountants with comprehensive expertise in Direct & Indirect Taxation, Audit, and Corporate Compliance.

We are committed to delivering personalised, technology-driven financial solutions to startups, MSMEs, professionals, and individuals across Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh, and India.
